About Cinemique

A movie browsing app with booking, seats, and admin-managed scheduling.

Cinemique helps users browse featured movies, watch trailers, open richer movie details, choose showtimes, select seats, and review booking history from schedules that admins control in one dashboard.

Featured monthly movies Custom movie descriptions Actors and directors DB-linked showtimes Seat booking history

What changed

Movie pages now show description, actors, directors, selectable showtimes, and seat-based booking flow.
The admin side now supports featured movies per month, validation feedback, hall assignment, payment status updates, search, and newest-first ordering.
Showtimes are stored in a dedicated table, bookings are tracked with payment states, and reports show occupancy plus top-performing titles.
